Our Mission

To revolutionize public transportation by providing real-time tracking solutions that empower commuters with reliable, accurate information about modern jeepney routes, reducing wait times and improving the overall travel experience for Filipino communities.

Our Vision

A future where every Filipino commuter has access to smart, connected public transportation. We envision communities where modern jeepneys are seamlessly integrated with technology, creating efficient, sustainable, and passenger-friendly transit systems that serve as the backbone of urban mobility.

The Story Behind Abang

Born from Daily Frustration

Every day, millions of Filipinos stand at jeepney stops, wondering when their ride will arrive. The uncertainty creates stress, wastes time, and affects productivity. Our team experienced this firsthand during our daily commutes.

We realized that while modern jeepneys are equipped with GPS technology, passengers had no way to access this information. The solution seemed obvious: bridge the gap between the technology already in vehicles and the people who need it most.

Abang was born from this simple yet powerful idea. By connecting passengers to real-time vehicle locations, we're not just building an app – we're creating peace of mind for every commuter who chooses public transportation.
